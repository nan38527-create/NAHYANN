Welcome to Grand Hotel

Established in 1995, Grand Hotel has been a cornerstone of luxury hospitality in Dubai. What started as a modest boutique hotel has evolved into one of the city's most prestigious accommodations, blending traditional Arabian hospitality with modern elegance.

Our Location

Strategically located in the heart of Dubai's business district, Grand Hotel offers unparalleled access to the city's most iconic landmarks. Just minutes away from Dubai Mall, Burj Khalifa, and the Dubai Metro, our prime location makes it the perfect base for both business travelers and tourists exploring the vibrant city.

Our Facilities

Experience world-class amenities at Grand Hotel. Our 200 elegantly appointed rooms and suites feature state-of-the-art technology, luxurious bedding, and stunning city views. Indulge in our award-winning restaurant serving international cuisine, relax at our rooftop pool with panoramic views, or rejuvenate at our full-service spa. Business travelers will appreciate our conference facilities and high-speed internet throughout the property.
