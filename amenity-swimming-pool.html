Infinity Swimming Pool

Experience luxury relaxation at our stunning rooftop infinity pool. Enjoy panoramic city views while swimming in our temperature-controlled pool or relaxing on our sun deck.

Pool Features

  • Infinity edge with city views
  • Temperature-controlled water
  • Poolside bar service
  • Luxury sun loungers
  • Towels and robe service
  • Shower and changing facilities

Opening Hours

Daily: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Pool Bar: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Safety Information

Children must be accompanied by adults. Lifeguard on duty during peak hours. Please follow posted safety guidelines.
